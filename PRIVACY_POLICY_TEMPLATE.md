# Privacy Policy for Cheeseburger Factory App

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## Introduction

Cheeseburger Factory ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

- **Account Information:** When you sign in with Google, we collect:
  - Name
  - Email address
  - Profile picture
  - Google account ID

- **Location Information:** With your permission, we collect:
  - Approximate location (to show nearby restaurant locations)
  - Selected restaurant location preference

- **Order Information:**
  - Order history
  - Favorite items
  - Loyalty points balance

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (device type, operating system)
- App usage data (features used, time spent)
- Log data (errors, crashes)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Account Management**
   - Creating and managing your account
   - Authenticating your identity

2. **Service Delivery**
   - Processing and tracking orders
   - Managing loyalty rewards
   - Showing nearby restaurant locations

3. **Communication**
   - Sending order confirmations
   - Notifying you about loyalty rewards
   - Responding to customer service requests

4. **Improvement**
   - Analyzing app usage to improve features
   - Fixing bugs and technical issues
   - Developing new features

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect your information:

### Google Sign-In
- **Purpose:** Authentication
- **Data Shared:** Name, email, profile picture
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Supabase
- **Purpose:** Database and backend services
- **Data Stored:** Account info, orders, loyalty points

- **Purpose:** External ordering platform

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We use Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure
- Authentication is handled through Google's secure OAuth system
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction:** Request corrections to inaccurate data
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Opt-Out:** Opt out of marketing communications
5. **Data Portability:** Request your data in a portable format

## Data Retention

- Account information: Retained while your account is active
- Order history: Retained for 2 years
- Deleted account data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Location Services

We request location permission to:
- Show nearby Cheeseburger Factory locations
- Provide distance information

You can disable location services in your device settings at any time. The app will continue to function, but you'll need to manually select your restaurant location.

## Cookies and Tracking

- Session cookies for authentication
